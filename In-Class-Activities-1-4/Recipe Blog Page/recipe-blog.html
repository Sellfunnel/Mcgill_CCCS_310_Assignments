Breakfast Recipes

Garlic Bread

This easy-to-make garlic bread recipe is perfect as a breakfast or snack option!

Ingredients: Bread, butter, garlic, parsley.

Instructions: Spread butter and garlic on bread, sprinkle parsley, and toast.



Lunch Recipes

Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian dish perfect for a family lunch. Serve with Parmesan cheese!

Ingredients: Spaghetti, ground beef, tomato sauce, onions, garlic, herbs.

Instructions: Cook pasta, prepare sauce, and combine. Serve hot!



Dinner Recipes

Grilled Salmon

A healthy and tasty dinner option. Best served with a side of vegetables.

Ingredients: Salmon fillets, olive oil, lemon, garlic, herbs.

Instructions: Marinate salmon, grill until cooked through, and serve with lemon slices.

Chocolate Cake

A rich and moist chocolate cake recipe that's perfect for dessert lovers!
